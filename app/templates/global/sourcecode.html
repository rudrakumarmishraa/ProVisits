Payment Gateway

Razorpay was used to build ProVisits. It helps processes online payments for online as well as offline businesses. Razorpay allows you to accept credit cards, debit cards, netbanking, wallet, and UPI payments with the Mobile App plugin. It uses a seamles integration, allowing the customer to pay on your website without being redirected away.
